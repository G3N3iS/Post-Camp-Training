From Khaki to Clarity

Post-Camp Training on Self-Development & Communication

About the Program

The From Khaki to Clarity program by Business Street Corner in partnership with SAED NYSC Katsina is a 3-month virtual training designed to help corps members build confidence, develop emotional intelligence, and master effective communication. Each session blends personal growth, leadership, and modern communication strategies, including the use of generative AI tools.

Meet the Facilitator

Mr. Emmanuel Akaluese

Mr. Emmanuel Akaluese is a leadership coach, accountant, and communications trainer passionate about helping young people discover purpose and articulate their potential. As the founder of Business Street Corner, he has trained hundreds of corps members and youth leaders across Nigeria in self-awareness, professional communication, and personal growth.
